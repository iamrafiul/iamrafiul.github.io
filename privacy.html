Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Vidforge ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the Vidforge application and website (collectively, the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

Vidforge is a personal content-creation tool. We collect minimal information necessary to operate the Service:

  • API Credentials: OAuth tokens and API keys you provide to connect your social media accounts (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook). These are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Content Data: Scripts, audio files, and videos generated by the Service are stored locally on your device.
  • Usage Logs: Basic operational logs (timestamps, error messages) are stored locally on your device for debugging purposes.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number) beyond what you voluntarily provide when contacting us.
  • Browsing or tracking data.
  • Analytics or telemetry data.
  • Data from your social media followers or audiences.

3. How We Use Information

The information handled by the Service is used solely to:

  • Authenticate with third-party platform APIs on your behalf to publish video content.
  • Generate and render video content as directed by you.
  • Maintain local logs for troubleshooting and operational monitoring.

4. Third-Party Services

The Service interacts with the following third-party platform APIs to publish content on your behalf:

  • TikTok — Content Posting API (governed by TikTok's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy)
  • YouTube — YouTube Data API (governed by YouTube's Terms of Service and Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Instagram — Instagram Graph API (governed by Instagram's Terms of Use and Privacy Policy)
  • Facebook — Facebook Graph API (governed by Facebook's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy)

We do not share your data with any other third parties. Your API credentials are used exclusively to interact with the platforms listed above and are stored only on your local device.

5. Data Storage and Security

All data processed by Vidforge — including API credentials, generated content, and operational logs — is stored locally on your device. We do not operate remote servers or cloud storage for user data. You are responsible for securing access to your device and the credentials stored on it.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over data retention. You may delete any data at any time by removing the relevant files from your device. Uninstalling the Service removes all locally stored data.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to address the situation.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict certain data processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

Since Vidforge stores all data locally on your device, you can exercise these rights directly by managing or deleting the files on your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at rafiul@iamrafiul.com.
